Welcome to Tainan, Taiwan

About Tainan

Tainan is a historic city located in southern Taiwan. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, traditional temples, and delicious street food.

Things to Do

  • Visit Chihkan Tower
  • Explore Anping Old Street
  • Sample local snacks at a night market

Getting There

Tainan is easily accessible by train or bus from major cities in Taiwan. The city also has its own airport, Tainan Airport.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or need assistance during your visit, please contact the Tainan Tourism Office at tourism@tainan.gov.tw
